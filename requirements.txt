# CFISD Rezoning Impact Analysis

An independent data science research project analyzing the statistical relationship between school rezoning decisions and academic outcomes in the **Cypress-Fairbanks Independent School District (CFISD)**, Cypress, Texas.

## Research Question

> Does school rezoning in CFISD significantly affect STAAR test performance and demographic composition, after controlling for socioeconomic factors?

## Methodology

- **Difference-in-Differences (DiD)** regression to estimate causal effects
- Compared rezoned schools (treatment) vs. non-rezoned schools (control) across 2016–2023
- Controlled for % economically disadvantaged students and median household income

## Features

- 📈 STAAR score trend analysis (treatment vs. control)
- 📊 Demographic shift tracking across school levels
- 📉 Regression coefficient visualization
- 🗺️ School-level comparison table
- 🧮 Interactive rezoning impact simulator

## Data Sources

- [Texas Education Agency](https://tea.texas.gov) — STAAR results, school demographics
- [CFISD District Reports](https://www.cfisd.net) — Rezoning documents
- [U.S. Census Bureau](https://www.census.gov) — Median income by tract

> ⚠️ The current version uses simulated data. Replace `data/staar_scores.csv` with real TEA exports for live research.
